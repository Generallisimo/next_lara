Auctions
and their advantages

The largest car auctions in the USA are Copart, Manheim and IAAI. These sites are 90% auctions where lots (cars) of the largest insurance companies of the USA are exhibited, with both minimal and significant damage.

«Copart» auction

Copart Insurance Auction is the largest car insurance auction, which sells vehicles in the condition "as is". This means that the auction staff, when accepting a car for sale, do not carry out any pre-sale preparation and sell it in the condition in which it has arrived. You can choose a car at the lowest price at this auction.

«Manheim» auction

Manheim is the closed insurance auction. A specialty of this organization is that only official dealers, as well as accredited companies, have the right to participate in the auction. As a general rule, lots on Manheim have no damage, are in perfect condition and do not require any repairs. Due to the limited access and condition of the lots, cars from this auction have the highest prices among the similar auctions.

«IAA-Insurance» auction

The IAA-Insurance auction also deals with the sale of vehicles that have been insured. You can find the cars among the lots as in the condition "as is" so prepared for sale. The auction has its own staff of mechanics who repair damaged cars. Consequentially, some models of vehicles can be more expensive only due to the fact that they have been repaired and are completely ready for operation.

Auto fit in the USA

Auto fit passes in several steps:

  • 1. We choose the best car options suitable to the customer
  • 2. We make a real estimate of the physical situation of the car
  • 3. We check the history of the car in the USA
  • 4. We check clean title to the car
  • 5. We check primary fault and participation in road traffic accident about which the seller can hide
  • 6. We check the seller
  • 7. We make an estimate of real value of the car taking into account all damage.

After a complete inspection of the car, we give an opinion up to what maximum bet is recommended to play at the auction.

Pictograms meanings on Copart

  • The badge indicates that at the time the vehicle was received at the Copart site, the auction specialists confirmed that the vehicle started, gears were switched on it, and it moved independently. However, there is no guarantee that this vehicle is roadworthy or legal to drive on a highway in any state. In other words, there is no guarantee that such a vehicle will pass all of the USA vehicle safety regulations. The most important information for us here is that the vehicle starts and drives.

  • Despite the translation of the term - from English it is something like "improved car" - this pictogram simply means that the car does not start and does not drive. Yes, Copart can service vehicles at the request of the insurance company or dealer: for example, it can wash cars, clean with a vacuum cleaner, apply anti-corrosion or other protective coating. However, these actions are not always implemented. But in most cases, this sign rather indicates that the car was preliminarily worked on and put in relative order. What does this mean for the buyer? If the car is subject to restoration, then there are many possible problems. We do not recommend buying here. But if the client needs a knock-down kit, then you should pay attention to such cars.

  • The badge with the English letter C is Seller's certification (guarantee): the buyer who purchases this lot is guaranteed that he will be satisfied with the purchase. If, for any reason, during the inspection of the vehicle or before the vehicle leaves the Copart site, the buyer makes a complaint, he may withdraw from the transaction without penalty or additional fees. Yes, in other situations there are fees and there are penalties.

  • The Copart pictogram with the letter C in a red circle is CrashedToys. It means that the lot is from the site of CrashedToys.

    For connoisseurs of the exotic. Vehicles with this mark are located at the sites of the CrashedToys company, which specializes in the sale of decommissioned motorcycles, exotic vehicles, motorhomes, boats. yachts, hydroscooters and other vehicles. Lots with this mark are accompanied by additional images or videos. In addition, the auction fees for such lots will differ upwards for you. The difference can be quite significant. If you are going to buy a motorcycle from America, then, believe me, you will see this pictogram very often. A huge number of motorcycles on Copart are sold through CrashedToys.

  • It means that at the time the vehicle was received by Copart, Copart representatives confirmed that the vehicle was running at idle speed. For a lot, such a mark indicates that the vehicle has problems with the suspension mount, or safety, or electronics, which makes it impossible to move on it, but the engine is intact. You need to look at specific lots, but vehicles with such a mark can be considered both for restoration and for disassembly in the form of a car knock-down kit.

  • Copart explains it as follows: popular cars that are in high demand among buyers. In fact, these are rather recommended vehicles with a bias towards expensive models in a good configuration and luxury cars, motorcycles for those who are not averse to adding to their collection. You can ignore this pictogram - there is little use.

  • Everything is very simple here. Such a mark denotes lots located on special sites indicated in the description. These vehicles are located outside of Copart sites. For example, the lot may be located in specially designated parking lots in the state. In turn, this may affect the cost of delivery from the auction to the port, or to our base for inspection before loading and shipping.

  • This icon used to mean that the car was auctioned under the FAST program - i.e. from franchise dealers specializing in vehicles from manufacturers such as Toyota, Honda and Ford. Cars under this franchise went to the dealer on account of paying a certain part of the amount when buying a new car. The lot itself at the time of the auction was at the dealer's sites and only in case of sale was delivered to Copart.
